Full Narration Script

Italic blue blocks = spoken VO. Grey blocks = screen direction for your reference only — do not read aloud.

Scene 1 — Hook
0:00 – 0:22
"What if your AI training actually felt like a mission? Prompt Ops Academy is a fully gamified, browser-based learning experience that teaches professionals how to write powerful AI prompts — through seven hands-on missions, real AI scoring, and a progression system that keeps learners coming back."
Screen: Hold on the animated welcome screen. Let the typewriter effect and HUD corner animations complete.
Scene 2 — Welcome & Enroll
0:22 – 0:50
"The experience opens with a cinematic Command Center interface — dark, focused, and immediately different from a typical e-learning platform. Learners enter their name and hit Enroll. No lengthy form, no role dropdown — just a fast path into Mission One."
Screen: Type "Jordan" into the name field, character by character. Click Enroll. Let the mission map load with staggered card animations.
Scene 3 — Command Center
0:50 – 1:18
"The Command Center shows all seven missions at once. The HUD at the top tracks XP earned, current level, and overall progress. Completed missions light up green; locked ones are dimmed until earned. This isn't decoration — the gamification is wired to actual performance. Learners advance by demonstrating skill, not by clicking Next."
Screen: Slowly pan over mission cards. Hover 2–3 to show the lift effect. Point cursor at XP counter and level badge in header.
Scene 4 — CLEAR Framework
1:18 – 1:50
"Inside a mission, learners meet the CLEAR framework — a five-element prompt structure that forms the backbone of the entire course. Command. Limits. Environment. Audience. Response. Each chip expands to explain one element with a practical example. This is the toolkit learners reference while they write."
Screen: Open Mission 1. Tap each CLEAR chip — C, L, E, A, R — letting each panel animate open before moving to the next.
Scene 5 — Prompt Lab
1:50 – 2:25
"Then comes the Prompt Lab — the heart of every mission. Learners get a real-world scenario: summarise a financial report for the board, draft a compliance notice, write an onboarding brief. They write their own AI prompt from scratch, drawing on the CLEAR framework chips for guidance. No multiple choice. No fill-in-the-blank. Actual prompt writing."
Screen: Scroll to the Prompt Lab. Show the scenario text. Auto-type a well-structured prompt into the textarea. Show the character counter updating.
Scene 6 — AI Scoring
2:25 – 2:50
"When the learner submits, a live AI scores the prompt across all five CLEAR elements — each rated zero to ten, colour-coded red, amber, or green. The total is out of fifty. The feedback is honest: a basic working prompt gets a two or a three. A properly structured prompt earns a four or five. No grade inflation."
Screen: Click Score. Show the loading state briefly. Reveal the results panel — score banner, five element chips, written analysis, and improvement suggestion.
Scene 7 — Mission Complete & Progression
2:50 – 3:20
"Score high enough and the mission completes with XP, a badge unlock, and a streak counter. Back in the Command Center, the completed mission lights up and the next one unlocks. Seven missions, each built around a different real-world prompt challenge — from board reports to HR compliance to team onboarding. The Grand Finale is an open AI sandbox where learners can keep practising without limits."
Screen: Show the Mission Complete screen with confetti. Click back to Command Center. Show the completed mission glowing green. Briefly open Mission 2. Pan to the Grand Finale card.
Scene 8 — Closing
3:20 – 3:35
"Prompt Ops Academy. Seven missions. Live AI feedback. Real skill, earned — not just clicked through. From beyondtalent, for DevLearn twenty twenty-six."
Screen: Slow zoom out to show the full Command Center. Hold. Fade to outro card.
